## About Me 

I'm a **Soil Science Researcher** passionate about understanding and solving soil degradation problems. My research focuses on developing sustainable amendment strategies for acidic and degraded soils in tropical and subtropical regions. When I'm not in the lab running XRD or FTIR analysis, you'll find me analyzing data in R or writing papers in LaTeX.

> *"The soil is the great connector of lives, the source and destination of all."* ? Wendell Berry

### What I Do
-  Investigate **soil acidification mechanisms** and develop amelioration strategies
-  Design and evaluate **biochar, lime, organic amendments, and composite soil conditioners**
-  Study **heavy metal stabilization** and nutrient cycling in amended soils
-  Explore **soil organic matter dynamics** and aggregate stability at multiple scales
-  Conduct **meta-analyses** to synthesize soil amendment research findings
