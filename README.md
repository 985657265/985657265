## About Me

Soil Science Researcher focusing on **soil amendment, remediation, and sustainable agriculture**. My work centers on developing and evaluating strategies for improving degraded and acidic soils in tropical and subtropical regions.

### Research Interests
- Soil acidification mechanisms and amelioration strategies
- Biochar, lime, organic amendments, and composite soil conditioners
- Heavy metal stabilization and nutrient retention in soils
- Soil organic matter dynamics and aggregate stability
- Meta-analysis and systematic review of soil amendment effects
